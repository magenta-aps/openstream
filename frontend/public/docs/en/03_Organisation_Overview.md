# 3. Organization overview

In this chapter, the organization overview page will be introduced. This page is sent to the user after login if they are an organization or sub-organization administrator, or if they are associated with more than one department as an employee.

The organization overview page provides an overview of all affiliated sub-organizations and departments. The page adapts dynamically based on your user role and the associated rights.

---

## 3.1 Employee view

As an **employee**, the overview of the departments you are associated with is displayed. To access a specific department, simply click **Select**.

![View for an employee with access to two departments](/docs/docs_images/en/employee_en_select_sub_org.png)

---

## 3.2 Sub-organization administrator view

As a **sub-organization administrator**, you have full access to all departments within your sub-organization as well as the option to create sub-organization templates. Your administrative rights include:
* Creation of new departments.
* Changing the names of departments.
* Deletion of existing departments.
* Creation of sub-organization templates

![View for an sub-organization administrator](/docs/docs_images/en/suborg_admin_en_select_sub_org.png)

---

## 3.3 Organization administrator view

As an **organization administrator**, you have the highest access rights and can see and manage all sub-organizations and departments. You have the same rights as a sub-organization administrator for all sub-organizations and can also create and delete the sub-organizations themselves.

![Visning for en organisationsadministrator](/docs/docs_images/en/org_admin_en_select_sub_org.png)
